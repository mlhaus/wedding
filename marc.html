About the Groom

Marc is a high school business and computer technology teacher in West Liberty, Iowa. He is also the webmaster of the West Liberty Community School District's web site, the West Liberty Chamber of Commerce's web site, as well as the web site that you are reading now (amy-marc.com).

Marc likes to cook dinner for Amy, watch movies, go tailgating during Iowa home games, play darts, play summer-league softball, and much more.

Marc graduated from the University of Northern Iowa with a degree in Business Teaching in December, 2003. After graduating, he spent approximately four months as a substitute teacher in Council Bluffs, Iowa before accepting his job in West Liberty.

Marc was born in Cedar Rapids, Iowa on February 6, 1981. He is the youngest of three children. His sister, Dana, lives in Marion, Iowa. She is married to Dave Medin and has two children, Dawson and Daniel. His brother, Craig, lives in Houston, Texas. He is on his ninth year of college at Rice University. In addition to going to school he teaches at North Harris College and conducts the Houston Youth Symphony Orchestra. You can read more about his mom and dad on the wedding party page.
